Information we collect

A) You provide information. When you freely disclose personal information to us through a number of features on the MyTurf, we may collect it. This contains your profile photo, phone number, and email address. By providing this information, you consent to our processing of that information, which you may cancel at any time.

Phone number (optional) - We needed a phone number to verify that the person was authentic. The user can also log in using their Apple ID or email address. For your safety, your phone number will not be displayed to other users.

Profile photo - Photo on your profile, allowing the user to be identified quickly. At the very least, the user agrees to supply a clean photo of his or her face. The use of the Services is contingent on both of these criteria being met. We're looking for a photo that shows a man or woman's face and isn't nudist.

B) Furthermore, while you use the MyTurf Services, you may be prompted to provide or send personal information to us, such as sending us comments via email. Our primary goal in collecting personal data from you is to provide you with a secure, seamless, efficient, and personalised experience. This enables us to offer services and features that are most likely to match your needs, as well as to tailor our service to make your experience safer and easier. We only collect personal information about you that we believe is required to accomplish this goal. You are no longer anonymous to us once you have registered, and you can examine and alter certain personal information under the Edit Profile settings section.

C) Geolocation Data. We'll utilise your location to figure out how far away you are from other users. Our servers keep your most recent known location in order to calculate distance information between you and other Users. While the MyTurf Service is active, we will also collect the geographic location data from your mobile device.
